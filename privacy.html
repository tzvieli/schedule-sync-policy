Privacy Policy

At Schedule Sync, your privacy and control over your Google data are our top priorities. Here’s exactly what we access and how we use it:

Data We Access

When you grant permission, we request only the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events
    Read and write access to your calendar events.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events.readonly
    Read-only access to prevent creating duplicate events.
  • openid profile email
    Basic profile info (your name and email) used solely for authentication.

How We Use Your Data

  • Calendar Events: We create, update, and read events to sync your college schedule directly into your Google Calendar.
  • Profile & Email: Used only to identify you and secure OAuth tokens.
  • No Third-Party Storage: All actions occur within Google; we do not store your events or profile data on our servers.
  • No Sharing or Selling: We never share, sell, or analyze your personal calendar data.

Security & Storage

  • All data is transmitted via HTTPS and OAuth 2.0 end-to-end encryption.
  • We leverage Google’s secure infrastructure—no event data is stored locally or on external servers.

Your Control

You can revoke or adjust permissions at any time in your Google Account Settings. To remove all access, uninstall the extension or revoke its OAuth access.

Third-Party Services

We integrate only with Google Calendar and Google OAuth. No other third parties touch your data.

Cookies & Tracking

We use only essential session cookies. We do not deploy analytics or tracking cookies.

Children’s Privacy

Intended for students age 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 16.
