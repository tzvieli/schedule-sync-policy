Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what we access, why, and how you stay in control.

1

Data We Access

Only the minimum OAuth scopes to:

  • Create & update calendar events from your schedule
  • Read events to prevent duplicates
2

How We Use It

All actions happen within Google:

  • No storage on our servers
  • No sharing, selling, or analyzing your events
3

Security

  • OAuth 2.0 & HTTPS encryption end-to-end
  • Leverage Google’s infrastructure—no local data
4

Your Control

Revoke or adjust permissions anytime in Google Account Settings, or uninstall the extension to remove all access.

5

Third-Party Services

We integrate only with Google. No other services touch your data.

6

Cookies

Essential cookies only for session management. Zero analytics or trackers.

7

Children’s Privacy

Intended for students 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8

Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally—check back here or follow us on Twitter.
