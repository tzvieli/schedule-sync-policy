Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here’s how Schedule Sync handles your data:

1. Data We Access

We only request the minimal scopes to:

  • Create and update calendar events based on your college schedule.
  • Read event lists to avoid duplicates.

2. Data Usage

All operations occur within your Google account. We:

  • Never store your calendar data on our servers.
  • Do not share, sell, or analyze your private events.

3. Security & Storage

All interactions go through Google's secure OAuth 2.0. We:

  • Use HTTPS end-to-end encryption.
  • Rely on Google’s trusted infrastructure—no local storage.

4. Your Control

You can revoke or adjust permissions any time via Google Account Settings .

To delete all integrations, uninstall the extension or revoke access.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any other services. Your data stays between you and Google.

6. Cookies & Tracking

This site uses only essential cookies for session management. No analytics or tracking cookies are deployed.

7. Children’s Privacy

Schedule Sync is intended for college students (age 16+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Check back here or follow us on Twitter for announcements.
