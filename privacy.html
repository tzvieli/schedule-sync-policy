Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Here’s exactly how Schedule Sync treats your data:

Data We Access

Minimal OAuth scopes to:

  • Create & update calendar events from your college schedule
  • Read existing events to prevent duplicates

How We Use Data

All actions happen within your Google account—no third-party storage or analysis:

  • No event data is stored on our servers
  • We never share, sell, or analyze your private events

Security & Storage

  • OAuth 2.0 and HTTPS encryption end-to-end
  • Leverage Google’s infrastructure—no local storage

Your Control

Revoke or adjust permissions anytime at Google Account Settings .

To remove all traces, simply uninstall the extension or revoke access.

Third-Party Services

We integrate only with Google. No other services touch your data.

Cookies & Tracking

Essential cookies only for session management. Zero analytics or trackers.

Children’s Privacy

Intended for students age 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Check back here or follow us on Twitter for announcements.
