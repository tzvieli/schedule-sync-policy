The Schedule Sync extension helps users sync their university schedule with their Google Calendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.

Information Collected

This extension does not collect or store any personal information on external servers.

Permissions Used

  • identity: To authenticate with your Google account and access your calendar.
  • scripting: To analyze the currently open university schedule page.
  • activeTab: To inject code into the active tab to extract schedule data.
  • host permissions: Required to run the extension on your university schedule site.

Calendar Access

The extension uses the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar scope to create and manage events in your Google Calendar. It only modifies events created by the extension and does not access your other calendar data.

Data Storage

All operations are performed locally in your browser. No data is sent to or stored by the developer.

Open Source

The extension is open-source. You can review the code on GitHub.
