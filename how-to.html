How to Use Schedule Sync

  1. Install the Schedule Sync Chrome Extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the extension icon and authorize with your Google account.
  3. Open your Moodle dashboard and click "Sync Courses" to sync your class schedule.
  4. Click "Sync Assignments" to sync all your Moodle assignments.
  5. Check your Google Calendar to see your updated schedule!
