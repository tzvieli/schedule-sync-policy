How to Use Schedule Sync

  1. Install the Schedule Sync Chrome Extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the extension icon and sign in with your Google account.
  3. Open your Moodle dashboard and click “Sync Courses” to import your class schedule.
  4. Click “Sync Assignments” to pull in all upcoming Moodle assignments.
  5. Check your Google Calendar—your courses and deadlines should now appear!
